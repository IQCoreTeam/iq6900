Advantages of uploading data on-chain.
Immutability:
Once data is recorded on the blockchain, it cannot be changed. This ensures data integrity, particularly important for proving ownership of documents or assets.
Transparency:
Since all data is stored on a public blockchain, anyone can access and verify it. This increases trustworthiness and reduces the risk of fraud or manipulation.
Decentralization:
Data can be stored and verified without a central authority, allowing for a trustworthy data management system without third-party intervention.
Proof of Ownership:
Can be used to prove ownership of digital assets or NFTs. Inscriptions make it clear which asset belongs to which address. Additionally, it allows proving the precedence of data, thereby creating value for digital multimedia.
